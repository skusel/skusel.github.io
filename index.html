Larry Skuse

Hi, I am Larry Skuse, and I am currently a senior software engineer at WH Trading LLC, which is a proprietary derivatives trading firm based in Chicago, IL.

Through my work, I have become well-versed in the development of post-trade processing software and trading systems.

In addition to the software I develop on the job, a notable portion of my time outside the office is spent writing software. I particularly enjoy working on tools and libraries that make other developers more effective and productive.

Prior to the start of my career, I earned a B.S. with honors in Computer Engineering from Milwaukee School of Engineering (MSOE). Several years later, in August of 2023, I earned a M.S. in Computer Science from Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech).

When I am not writing software, I enjoy being outside. You can often find me running, cycling, golfing, skiing, or in various local rec leagues. I am a Chicago sports fan, an avid reader of both historical fiction and non-fiction, and an amateur travel photographer. You can view some of my favorite travel photography in my Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, and South America photo albums.
